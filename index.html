What is it?

dbdeploy is a Database Change Management tool. It's for developers or DBAs who want to evolve their database design – or refactor their database – in a simple, controlled, flexible and frequent manner.

Why do I need it?

The recurring problem with database development is that at some point you'll need to upgrade an existing database and preserve its content. In development environments it's often possible (even desirable) to blow away the database and rebuild from scratch as often as the code is rebuilt but this approach cannot be taken forward into more controlled environments such as QA, UAT and Production.

How do I use it?

Drawing from our experiences, we've found that one of the easiest ways to allow people to change the database is by using version-controlled SQL delta scripts. We've also found it beneficial to ensure that the scripts used to build development environments are the exact same scripts used in QA, UAT and production. Maintaining and making use of these deltas can quickly become a significant overhead – dbdeploy aims to address this.

dbdeploy automatically keeps track of which delta scripts have been applied to your databases, and helps you incrementally change your database over time without having to resort to big-bang approaches
